Our office location and family business story

Our Office Location

Operating for several years in various locations as the only fully independent financial planning and investment firm in Northern Ontario we found a permanent home when we purchased the historic Barry Property on Bellevue Avenue shown below, located on an acre of land on the shores of Minnow Lake, which had just become available on the passing of Mrs. Barry, a pioneer resident of the area at age 89 in 2000. This property was zoned commercial so was ideal for our purpose although renovations were necessary to the home which was built in the 1930's. It was conveniently located close to our residence which made it even more attractive as we can walk to work. Our clients appreciate the warm and welcoming atmosphere of this fine old building.

The office is located at 1546 Bellevue Avenue close to the corner of Bancroft Drive and Bellevue Avenue in Minnow Lake as shown on the map above. Watch for signs on the fence. Office hours are weekdays 9 to 5 with appointments after hours.

A Family Business

Financial Decisions is locally owned and operated by the husband and wife team of John Lindsay and Linda Cartier and daughter Melanie Cartier. The firm recently received a 25 year membership plaque in the Greater Sudbury Chamber of Commerce. Linda is President of the company since its formation and has had extensive experience in the financial services industry and as a community volunteer together with her partners.

As strong advocate for higher standards in financial planning, she taught the CFP (Certified Financial Planner) Course at Cambrian College and financial education courses for the general public. Linda was the first financial planner in Northeastern Ontario to hold both the CFP (Certified Financial Planner) and R.F.P. (Registered Financial Planner) designations. She is also a PRP (Professional Retirement Planner) and an ELP (Enhanced Lifestyle Planner) and is a CFDS (Chartered Financial Divorce Specialist). She is the proprietor of the Academy of Financial Divorce Specialists that trains Canadian qualified financial professionals to help those dealing with financial matters in separation and divorce. The website is www.afds.ca for more information.

She has been involved in numerous volunteer activities and received awards for this work and continues to support local initiatives as do both Melanie and John.
