  <h2 style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #234e75; font-size: 1.75rem; font-weight: 700; line-height: 1.15;">
    Caring for Your Family
  </h2>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    Family fun brings visions to mind of times together celebrating special occasions, playing games or outings in the great outdoors, plus enjoying meals together. Sometimes when we think of family, it is the time taking care of a member who has fallen sick or needs support to be successful.
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    What is not as frequently thought of is "taking care of business" for family to ensure you do not create a mess for members to clean up. During our meetings with you, we have been checking on your estate planning to ensure you have the necessary documents in place to deal with either incapacity or death. These are not the fun parts of life, so often they are put on the back burner rather than making these tasks a priority.
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    Once a person is over 18, they need a Power of Attorney (POA) for personal care, and if they have assets, they also need one for Property. Ideally, a will would be created to have your wishes identified to carry out too. We also need copies of your POA for Property so we can help your family if the situation arises.
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    The POAs are available from: https://www.ontario.ca/page/make-power-attorney
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    Do not wait to act on putting these documents in place - it is the kindest thing you can do for your family.
  </p>
</section>

  <h2 style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #8b3d3d; font-size: 1.65rem; font-weight: 700; line-height: 1.15;">
    More Important Fraud Information
  </h2>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    We have been advised by some investment fund companies and banks that fraud scams are increasing. They are in the form of emails, text messages, brochures in the mail and phone calls. Please check the email address for small details before opening. Sometimes there is an extra letter or period that has been added by the scammer.
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    If you receive a phone call or text and someone is asking you for money, even a family member, be cautious. If you know the person calling and asking for money, it is best to hang up and call your family member back using the contact information you have.
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    As our client, we have a relationship with you, and you know the nature of our phone calls and emails. We will either call or email regarding appointments, confirming decisions about your investments, taxes, insurance, or about any other service we already provide to you.
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    Your call display will say Financial Decisions on a landline or cell phone. We have been told by some clients that their cell phone is showing the Academy of Financial Divorce Specialists (which is a separate sister company out of the same office address).
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 0.9rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
    Our office number is 705-525-7526. If any name other than Financial Decisions comes up, this is a digital error that can only be changed by you on your cell phone.
  </p>
  <p style="margin: 0 0 1rem; color: #334155; line-height: 1.85;">
